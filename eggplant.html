Eggplant

Eggplant needs warm conditions, and will not thrive during a cool season. There are varieties are available that thrive with Minnesota’s short summer season.

The varity of seeds and its types is growing bigger and wildly, although the technology is improving globally, but the experience is still required for getting benefitial fruits

Improve your soil by adding well-rotted manure or compost in spring or fall. Too much nitrogen fertilization will lead to plants that are bushy, leafy and slow to bear fruit.
